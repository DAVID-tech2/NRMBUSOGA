Who We Are

Empowering Busoga With Unity & Progress

The Busoga Region NRM Volunteers and Mobilizers Association is dedicated to uniting communities, strengthening the NRM agenda, and driving development through grassroots mobilization.

Our team works tirelessly to ensure that Busoga's voice is heard, supporting government programs, and fostering peace, stability, and prosperity across the region.
